What is Goiaba

Goiaba is an open-source hardware development board built around the Espressif ESP32-S3, designed for seamless cloud-powered NLP and AIoT applications. It sports a high-sensitivity MEMS microphone with an I²S audio interface for crystal-clear voice capture, plus an optional speaker output for instant feedback. Audio streams securely over Wi-Fi to your AI agent in real time—enabling on-demand speech recognition, intent detection, and complex data processing without taxing the board itself.

Features

  • Dual-core ESP32-S3 with neural acceleration
  • High-sensitivity MEMS microphone (I²S)
  • Optional speaker output
  • Wi-Fi & Bluetooth connectivity
  • Camera interface for external camera modules
  • Multiple GPIO pins for sensors and actuators
